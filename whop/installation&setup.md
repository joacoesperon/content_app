Installation & Setup

Welcome to Jess Trading Bot!
Follow these simple steps to set everything up correctly.



🔹 1️⃣ Create Your Broker Account

To ensure optimal performance and identical trading hours, we recommend using IC Trading.

👉Create your account here: https://www.ictrading.com/en/register/live





Choose MT5 as your platform.



Verify your account.



Fund it with your desired amount.

(You can start with as little as $300, but $1000+ is recommended for best risk management.)



🔹 2️⃣ Download and Install MetaTrader 5

If you don’t already have it:

👉 Download MT5 here: https://secure.ictrading.com/Downloads

Once installed:





Log in with your broker credentials.



Make sure you’re connected to your live or demo account.



🔹 3️⃣ Download the Files

Go to the Files section inside Whop.
You’ll find two files:





RangeBreakoutBot_v1.0.ex5 → The Expert Advisor file to attach in MT5.



Range Breakout Bot – Setup Guide.pdf → Explains each parameter and the strategy logic.



🔹 4️⃣ Load the Bot in MT5





Open MT5.



Click File → Open Data Folder.



Go to MQL5 → Experts and paste the .ex5 file there.



Restart MT5.



Open a XAUUSD (Gold) chart in the 1-minute or 5-minute timeframe.



Drag the bot from the Navigator → Expert Advisors section onto the chart.



Enable AutoTrading (top toolbar).

🔹 5️⃣ VPS (Optional but Recommended)

For 24/7 automated trading without keeping your computer on:





In MT5, right-click your account → Register a Virtual Server (VPS).



Follow the setup wizard.



Synchronize your chart and bot.
