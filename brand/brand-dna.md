BRAND OVERVIEW
Name: Jess Trading
Tagline: "The future of trading is automated."
Design Agency: In-house
Voice Adjectives: Premium, Confident, Approachable, Clear, Visionary

VISUAL SYSTEM
Primary Font: Inter (Google Font) — Bold for headlines/CTAs, Regular/Medium for body, Light for secondary elements. Max two weights per design.
Secondary Font: N/A (Inter only, different weights)
Primary Color: Carbon Black #101010 (radial gradient from #101010 center to #010101 exterior)
Secondary Color: Neon Green #45B14F (luminous variant #A5F28C for highlights)
Accent Color: Electric Blue #2979FF (reserved exclusively for CTAs and links)
Background Colors: Carbon Black radial gradient (always), never flat black
Text Colors: White for primary text, Light Gray #A7A7A7 / #D7D7D7 for secondary text and body copy
CTA Color and Style: Electric Blue #2979FF buttons, clean rounded corners, high contrast against dark backgrounds

PHOTOGRAPHY DIRECTION
Lighting: Dark, moody, premium — think Apple keynote presentation lighting. High contrast with selective illumination.
Color Grading: Cool darks with selective neon green highlights. Deep blacks, minimal midtones.
Composition: Grid-based, generous negative space, strong contrast. Minimalist fintech aesthetic.
Subject Matter: Candlestick charts, abstract tech visuals, finance-related graphics, trading interfaces, automation concepts.
Props and Surfaces: Clean tech surfaces, screens with trading data, abstract geometric patterns.
Mood: Premium, futuristic, trustworthy, sophisticated.
